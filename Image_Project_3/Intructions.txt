**THE ABACHA PROJECT**

Use the following guidelines while working on the project:

* Prmary color: #f05334

* Font Family: 'Segoe UI', Tahoma, Geneva, Verdana, sans-serif

* Use the line height property with a value of 4rem to space out the text content 

* The navbar must be fixed at the top of the page while every other content goes under it when you scroll
