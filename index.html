About The Dish

African Salad (locally known as Abacha) is one of the meals mostly prepared and enjoyed by the Igbo speaking people of Nigeria. Its native taste is so distinctive, making it a delicacy to be enjoyed by all. It is uaually served as a side dish in special events such as traditional weddings,funerals, anniversaries or the visit of a speacial guest. It is best served with Palm Wine, a local drink tapped from special Palm Trees, but you can also enjoy it with any chilled drink of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 300g Ababcha (Dried shredded Cassava)
  • 100g Ugba (Ukapaka)
  • 200ml Palm Oil
  • 2 tablespoonful of ground crayfish
  • 1 teaspoonful of smoothly grounded potash
  • 1 cube of seasoning
  • 1 large (or 2 small onions)
  • Chilli Pepper or any hot pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 100g of finely chopped garden egg leaves
  • Boiled dried fish or Stockfish
